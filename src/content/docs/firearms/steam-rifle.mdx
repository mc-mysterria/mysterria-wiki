### Overview

The Steam-Pressured Rifle is a steam-powered masterpiece designed for long-distance elimination. With **100-block range** and **14.0 base damage**, it's the most powerful mystical firearm available.

<Aside type="caution">
Parts for this weapon are obtained as **rewards from dungeons (nodes)**. Features authentic Kar98k-style reload mechanics.
</Aside>

### Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**

- **Highest damage** - 20.0 per shot
- **Extreme range** - 100 blocks for sniper-style combat
- **One-shot potential** - Can kill most mobs in 1-2 hits
- **Tactical reload** - Interruptible Kar98k-style loading
- **Precision weapon** - Perfect for long-range elimination

**Weaknesses:**

- **Slowest fire rate** - 1.3 seconds between shots
- **Longest full reload** - 4 seconds (though interruptible)
- **Smallest magazine** - Only 5 rounds
- **Most expensive ammo** - Rifle rounds cost 2 brass per 6 cases

### How to Obtain

Steam-Pressured Rifle parts can be obtained in dungeons and overworld chest. You cannot craft these parts.

### Required Parts

**5 Parts Required:**
- Rifle Barrel
- Rifle Stock
- Rifle Scope
- Rifle Pipe Assembly
- Mechanical Power Box

### Acquisition Steps

1. **Complete dungeon nodes** on the Mysterria server
2. **Collect all 5 parts** as rewards (may take multiple runs)
3. **Craft a Weaponsmith Table** if you don't have one
4. **Assemble the weapon** by shift-right-clicking the Weaponsmith Table with all parts in inventory

<Aside type="tip">
The Steam-Pressured Rifle is the rarest weapon. Coordinate with your team to efficiently farm dungeon nodes!
</Aside>

### Reload Mechanics

The Steam-Pressured Rifle features **Kar98k-style reload mechanics** with sequential loading.

### How Kar98k-Style Reload Works

- **One at a time:** Each round loads individually
- **Interruptible:** You can **stop reloading and fire** at any moment
- **Flexible:** Load 1-5 rounds depending on tactical needs
- **Authentic:** Inspired by real Kar98k bolt-action rifles

### Reload Timing

**Full Reload:** 4 seconds for 5 rounds (~0.8 seconds per round)

| Rounds to Load | Approximate Time |
|----------------|------------------|
| 1 round | ~0.8 seconds |
| 2 rounds | ~1.6 seconds |
| 3 rounds | ~2.4 seconds |
| 4 rounds | ~3.2 seconds |
| 5 rounds (full) | ~4.0 seconds |

<Aside type="tip">
**Sniper Reload Strategy:** After firing a killing shot, immediately start reloading. If another target appears, you can fire your partially-loaded rounds!
</Aside>

### Combat Guide

### Best Use Cases

**Long-Range Sniping (50-100 blocks):**
- Eliminate targets before they can respond
- Perfect accuracy at extreme distances
- One-shot capability on most enemies

**Precision Strikes:**
- Take out high-priority targets first
- Eliminate threats before close combat
- Support teammates from the backline

### Tactical Tips

<Aside type="tip">
**Master Positioning:** The rifle excels at 60-100 blocks. Position yourself where enemies can't easily reach you.
</Aside>

**Pre-Combat:**
- Find elevated or protected positions
- Load Implosive or Exorcist rounds for first shots
- Ensure clear sight lines to targets
- Mark target priority (bosses, archers, mages)

**During Combat:**
- **ALWAYS stand still** when firing (perfect accuracy required)
- Fire from cover or at extreme range
- Reload behind cover or during enemy downtime
- Don't panic - quality over quantity with 5 rounds

**Ammunition Selection:**
- **Boss Fights:** Implosive Rounds (17.5 damage!)
- **Undead Sniping:** Exorcist Rounds (35.0 damage vs undead!)
- **Armored Targets:** Armor-Piercing Rounds
- **General Sniping:** Standard Rifle Rounds

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can the Steam-Pressured Rifle one-shot players?**

A: Against unarmored or lightly armored players, yes! You can one-shot players without armor or with weak armor. Diamond/Netherite armor requires 2-3 shots.

**Q: Is the slow fire rate a problem?**

A: Not if you use it correctly! The rifle is designed for **precision, not volume**. Position properly, make your shots count, and the 14.0 damage compensates for slower fire rate.

**Q: How much does rifle ammunition cost?**

A: Rifle rounds are expensive:
- 6 cases require 2 brass
- 6 cores require 1 iron ingot + 1 brass
- **Total: 3 brass + 1 iron ingot for 6 rounds**

**Q: Can I use this in close combat?**

A: Not recommended! The rifle excels at 60-100 blocks. In close combat:
- Slow fire rate is a liability
- Long reload is dangerous
- Accuracy suffers while moving
- Carry a Deathknell or Klein's Repeater as backup
