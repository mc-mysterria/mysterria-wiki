### Ammunition System

Every mystical firearm supports 13 different ammunition variants with unique effects. All ammo types use the same base damage as the weapon, but special rounds have damage modifiers and mystical effects.

### Ammunition Types Overview

**Standard Ammunition:**

| Ammo Type | Damage | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Klein Rounds** | 100% | Standard repeater ammunition |
| **Deathknell Rounds** | 100% | Standard revolver ammunition |
| **Rifle Rounds** | 100% | Standard rifle ammunition |

**Special Ammunition Effects:**

**Fire Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 85% of base
- **Effect:** Ignites target for 5 seconds
- **Best Against:** Enemies vulnerable to fire, clearing groups
- **Tactical Use:** Area denial and damage over time

**Poison Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 85% of base
- **Effect:** Poisons target for 8 seconds
- **Best Against:** High-health enemies, sustained combat
- **Tactical Use:** Weakening tough opponents over time

**Armor-Piercing Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 90% of base
- **Effect:** Ignores 10 points of armor
- **Best Against:** Heavily armored players and mobs
- **Tactical Use:** Countering diamond/netherite armor

**Aging Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 85% of base
- **Effect:** Slowness I + Wither II (10 seconds)
- **Best Against:** Fast-moving enemies, kiting strategies
- **Tactical Use:** Slowing down aggressive opponents

**Spirit Control Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 85% of base
- **Effect:** Slowness IV + Blindness (5 seconds)
- **Best Against:** Players and intelligent mobs in PvP
- **Tactical Use:** Disabling enemies for follow-up attacks

**Deceiving Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 85% of base
- **Effect:** Rotates target 180 degrees
- **Best Against:** Players in PvP, disorienting enemies
- **Tactical Use:** Causing confusion and positional advantage

**Deprivation Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 85% of base
- **Effect:** Removes all positive status effects
- **Best Against:** Buffed enemies and potion users
- **Tactical Use:** Countering enemy buffs in PvP

**Exorcist Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 100% base, 250% against undead
- **Effect:** Massive bonus damage to zombies, skeletons, phantoms, withers
- **Best Against:** Undead mobs, Nether exploration
- **Tactical Use:** Specialized undead hunting ammunition

**Implosive Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 125% of base (highest damage)
- **Effect:** Pure devastating damage with no status effects
- **Best Against:** Bosses, high-priority targets, burst damage
- **Tactical Use:** Maximum single-shot damage

**Rotten Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 85% of base
- **Effect:** Hunger III + Weakness I (8 seconds)
- **Best Against:** Players in PvP scenarios
- **Tactical Use:** Depleting enemy resources and combat effectiveness

**Weakening Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 85% of base
- **Effect:** Weakness II + Mining Fatigue II (10 seconds)
- **Best Against:** Enemies you want to debuff for teammates
- **Tactical Use:** Support fire in group combat

**Demon Hunter Rounds:**
- **Damage:** 100% base, 250% against Beyonders
- **Effect:** Massive bonus damage to Beyonder mobs (from CircleOfImagination plugin)
- **Best Against:** Beyonder enemies on the server
- **Tactical Use:** Specialized anti-Beyonder combat

Special ammunition is crafted by surrounding the effect material with 8 standard rounds (of any weapon type).

**Recipe Pattern:**

Place the effect material in the center of a crafting table and surround it with 8 standard rounds to create 8 special effect rounds.

**Effect Materials:**

| Effect Material | Special Ammunition | Effect |
|----------------|-------------------|--------|
| **Blaze Powder** | Fire Rounds | Ignites for 5s |
| **Spider Eye** | Poison Rounds | Poisons for 8s |
| **Iron Ingot** | Armor-Piercing | Ignores 10 armor |
| **Clock** | Aging Rounds | Slowness I + Wither II (10s) |
| **Soul Sand** | Spirit Control | Slowness IV + Blindness (5s) |
| **Ender Pearl** | Deceiving Rounds | Rotates 180Â° |
| **Milk Bucket** | Deprivation Rounds | Removes buffs |
| **Glowstone Dust** | Exorcist Rounds | 250% vs undead |
| **TNT** | Implosive Rounds | 125% damage |
| **Rotten Flesh** | Rotten Rounds | Hunger III + Weakness I (8s) |
| **Fermented Spider Eye** | Weakening Rounds | Weakness II + Mining Fatigue II (10s) |
| **Nether Star** | Demon Hunter Rounds | 250% vs Beyonders |

<Aside type="tip">
**Universal Compatibility:** You can use ANY type of standard rounds (Klein, Deathknell, or Rifle) to craft special ammunition. The special effect applies to all weapon types!
</Aside>

### Ammunition Strategy

**Loadout Recommendations:**

**For General Exploration:**
- Carry standard rounds for regular combat
- Pack Exorcist rounds for undead encounters
- Bring Fire rounds for area control

**For PvP Combat:**
- Spirit Control rounds for disabling
- Deceiving rounds for disorientation
- Deprivation rounds to counter buffs
- Implosive rounds for burst damage

**For Boss Fights:**
- Implosive rounds for maximum damage
- Weakening rounds for debuffing
- Armor-Piercing for tanky bosses

**For Nether/End:**
- Exorcist rounds for the Nether (undead)
- Fire rounds are less effective in the Nether
- Demon Hunter rounds if Beyonders are present

**Resource Management:**

<Aside type="caution">
Special rounds require 8 standard rounds + rare material = 8 special rounds. Plan accordingly!
</Aside>

**Cost Breakdown for 8 Special Rounds:**
- 8 Standard Rounds (each requires: 1 Case + 1 Core + 1 Gunpowder)
- 1 Effect Material (Nether Star is the rarest)

**Total Materials for 8 Special Rounds:**
- 8 Cartridge Cases
- 8 Cartridge Cores
- 8 Gunpowder
- 1 Effect Material
