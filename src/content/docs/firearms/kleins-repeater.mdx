### Overview

Klein's Repeater is a balanced, reliable firearm perfect for beginners and versatile enough for experienced users. It's the **only weapon you can craft from scratch**, making it your gateway into mystical firearms.

---

### Statistics

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Type** | Mystical Repeater |
| **Damage** | 4.0 per shot |
| **Magazine Size** | 6 rounds |
| **Range** | 40 blocks |
| **Reload Time** | 3 seconds (full magazine) |
| **Fire Rate** | 1 shot per second |
| **Ammo Type** | Klein Rounds (all 13 variants supported) |
| **Reload Type** | Full Magazine |
| **Accuracy (Standing)** | Perfect (0° spread) |
| **Accuracy (Moving)** | 4° spread |
| **Accuracy (In Air)** | 8° spread |

---

### Strengths & Weaknesses

**Strengths:**

- **Craftable from scratch** - No dungeon farming required
- **Balanced stats** - Good for most combat situations
- **Fast fire rate** - 1 shot per second allows rapid follow-up shots
- **Moderate range** - 40 blocks covers most combat encounters
- **Easy to maintain** - Simple ammunition crafting

**Weaknesses:**

- **Lower damage** - Only 4.0 per shot (lowest of all weapons)
- **Medium range** - Outclassed by rifle at long distances
- **Full magazine reload** - Cannot interrupt reload to fire
- **Smaller magazine** - 6 rounds vs Deathknell's tactical reload

### Combat Guide

### Best Use Cases

**Close to Medium Range Combat (10-40 blocks):**
- Rapid fire for sustained damage
- Effective against groups with Fire rounds
- Good for mobile combat

**PvP Scenarios:**
- Spirit Control rounds for disabling
- Deceiving rounds for disorientation
- Implosive rounds for burst damage

**Early Game Progression:**
- First weapon before obtaining dungeon rewards
- Reliable damage output for exploration
- Easy ammunition sustainability

### Tactical Tips

<Aside type="tip">
**Reload Management:** Klein's Repeater has a 3-second full reload. Find cover or create distance before reloading!
</Aside>

**Pre-Combat:**
- Load Implosive or Exorcist rounds for first shots
- Ensure full magazine before engagement
- Position at 20-35 blocks for optimal accuracy

**During Combat:**
- Stand still when firing for perfect accuracy
- Use all 6 rounds before reloading
- Switch to melee if caught during reload

**Ammunition Selection:**
- **General Exploration:** Standard Klein Rounds
- **Undead Hunting:** Exorcist Rounds (250% damage)
- **Armored Enemies:** Armor-Piercing Rounds
- **Boss Fights:** Implosive Rounds (125% damage)

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use Deathknell Rounds in Klein's Repeater?**

A: No, Klein's Repeater only accepts Klein Rounds. However, all 13 special ammunition effects work with Klein Rounds!

**Q: How much damage does Klein's Repeater deal with Implosive Rounds?**

A: Implosive Rounds deal 125% damage, so: 4.0 × 1.25 = **5.0 damage per shot**

**Q: Is Klein's Repeater good for PvP?**

A: Yes! While it has lower damage, the fast fire rate and support for special ammunition (Spirit Control, Deceiving, Deprivation) make it effective in PvP.

**Q: Can I interrupt the reload?**

A: No, Klein's Repeater uses full magazine reload mechanics. You cannot fire until the 3-second reload completes.

**Q: What's the best ammunition type for Klein's Repeater?**

A: It depends on your situation:
- **Exorcist Rounds** for undead (10.0 damage vs undead!)
- **Implosive Rounds** for bosses (5.0 damage)
- **Spirit Control Rounds** for PvP
- **Standard Klein Rounds** for general use
