### Godhood Madness

As a Beyonder ascends beyond Sequence 4, their divine nature begins to **overpower their humanity**. This manifests as a
constant, baseline level of **Madness** that cannot be reduced through normal means — not by time, not by ordinary
spells.

This is **Godhood Madness**, and it scales with your Sequence:

| Sequence | Permanent Godhood Madness |
|----------|---------------------------|
| 4        | 25%                       |
| 3        | 40%                       |
| 2        | 60%                       |
| 1        | 75%                       |
| 0        | 95%                       |

Unlike regular Madness, this form **cannot be healed by any standard method**. The only way to counterbalance it is
through the **Church system** — grounding your divine nature in the humanity of your followers.

---

### Creating Your Church

Starting from **Sequence 4**, a new option called **Churches** becomes available inside your **Mystic Arts** menu. From
here, you can found your own Church — a congregation of players who acknowledge your divine authority.

:::tip[A church is not optional at high Sequences]
Without followers, the Godhood Madness will accumulate and push you toward mutation. A Church is a necessity, not a
luxury.
:::

---

### Gaining Followers (Anchors)

Followers are recruited through your **Honorific Name**. When another player chants your Honorific Name, you will have
the option to **invite them into your Church**. If they accept, they become your **Anchor**.

Each Anchor slightly **reduces your Godhood Madness**. The number of followers required to fully counteract the madness
of each Sequence:

| Sequence | Godhood Madness | Anchors needed to fully mitigate |
|----------|-----------------|----------------------------------|
| 4        | 25%             | ~12                              |
| 3        | 40%             | ~20                              |
| 2        | 65%             | ~28                              |
| 1        | 75%             | ~36                              |
| 0        | 95%             | ~50                              |

:::caution[Commitment is mutual]
Once a player accepts your invitation as an Anchor, **they cannot leave your Church for one week**. In return, they
receive meaningful bonuses for their loyalty.
:::

---

### Follower Bonuses

Being part of a Church is not purely a service to the divine — followers gain real benefits:

- **Pathway bonus** — If you are a follower in a Church whose leader shares your **same pathway**, you receive an **additional bonus to your passive acting**. Your connection to a more advanced being on the same path accelerates your
  own growth.

---

### Angels

If your Church contains players who have an **active Beyonder system** (i.e. they are at Sequence 4 or below, not yet
Sequence 5+), you may designate them as your **Angel**.

Angels have special responsibilities and privileges:

- They can **invite new players** into your Church on your behalf, expanding your congregation without requiring a
  direct Honorific Name chant
- In exchange, they receive a **fraction of your Anchor count** applied toward reducing their own Madness — a reward for
  serving as your divine intermediaries

:::note[Angels share your burden]
The more diligently your Angels recruit, the more your Church grows — and the more both of you benefit. It is a
relationship of mutual spiritual investment.
:::
