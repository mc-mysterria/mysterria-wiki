### Churches Are Now Living Institutions

The age of quiet faith is over.

Churches stop being names hidden inside a menu and become living powers in the world. A Church is now shaped by its members, their seniority, their piousness, their offerings, their active sites, their blessings, and the health of every holy place bound to its name.

---

### Founding a Church

Every church begins with a core.

Founders place and bind physical church cores into the world. These cores are the first altar of authority. The crafting recipe becomes available to you when you are eligible to make a Church.

**To found a church:**
1. Obtain a Church Core
2. Place it in the world at your chosen location
3. Bind it through the founding flow in the Mystic Arts menu
4. Name your church (enter the name in chat when prompted)
5. Build outward from the core into a real sacred site


:::caution[Cooldown applies]
If you recently left or were removed from a church, you must wait 7 days before joining or founding another.
:::

### Sacred Sites

The church site system supports three major site types:

- **Main church sites** — the heart of the church, where the core resides
- **Branch sites** — extensions of the faith into other territories
- **Regional headquarters** — the administrative seat of a wider diocese

**How to use them:**
- Place your main site where the core is bound
- Add branch sites to extend your reach and capacity
- Establish regional headquarters to support a wider sacred network

Sites contribute to capacity, stability, progression, perk scaling, and church activity. Expand your sacred geography and your church can support more believers. Neglect it, or let enemies break it, and the foundation of your influence begins to crack.

### How Churches Grow

Church progression is built around active participation. A church grows because people serve it, not because it exists on paper.

**The inputs that matter:**
- **Piousness** — members actively praying and participating in rites
- **Seniority** — long-term members contribute more weight
- **Role coverage** — bishops, deacons, and wardens assigned and active
- **Site activity** — sacred ground being maintained and used
- **Saturation** — how fully your sites are utilized
- **Offerings** — contributions to the church treasury
- **Ritual support** — participation in ceremonies
- **Prayer support** — regular prayer directed at the church and its leader
- **Network health** — overall condition of all sites bound to the church

**What to do as a leader:**
- Recruit active members, not just warm bodies
- Assign roles so the church functions (bishops, deacons, wardens)
- Keep sites maintained and defended
- Encourage regular offerings and prayer
- Monitor the Church Status screen for Active Anchor Weight vs Anchors Needed

**What to do as a member:**
- Pray regularly at your church's sites
- Participate in rituals and ceremonies
- Make offerings to the treasury
- Stay active
— inactive members (14+ days) stop counting as anchors

The result is a church that grows through devotion. Members who pray, contribute, stay loyal, and remain active help shape the church's power. Leaders now maintain an institution, not a passive bonus machine.

### Scaled Church Perks

Church benefits now scale through a dedicated perk system rather than flat rewards.

Perks can affect acting, madness recovery, blessings, prayer support, support gifts, and church stability. These bonuses answer to the condition of the church: how active it is, how healthy its sacred network is, how experienced its members are, and whether its roles are properly covered.

A small church can still survive. A maintained church with active members and protected sites becomes something else entirely: a force other factions have to respect.

---

### Prayer, Blessings, and Support Gifts

Churches now become shelters against the darker parts of the Beyonder path.

Through prayer and blessing systems, churches can offer practical aid to their followers. Support gifts include:
- Madness relief
- Spirituality restoration
- Tiredness recovery
- Sanctuary renewal

**How to use prayer:**
- Chant the leader's complete Honorific Name line by line in chat
- The system tracks your progress; if you break the chain, it resets
- Complete the full chant to offer a prayer

**How blessings work:**
- Leaders can bless followers through the Church GUI
- Blessed members receive support gifts based on church perk scaling
- These are non-combat tools with costs, burdens, and limits

A church can steady a shaken mind, restore a wounded spirit, and renew a sanctuary, but it cannot simply erase the price of battle.

### Artifact Vaults

Churches now have artifact vault systems.

Leaders can manage church artifact storage, artifact requests, vault protection, and vault-related recipes.

**To use the vault:**
- Access it through the Church GUI (leader/angels only)
- Store Sealed Artifacts and relics for the institution
- Members can submit artifact requests for leader approval
- Vault recipes unlocks are protected — breaking in has consequences

---

### Diplomacy and War

Church politics now have teeth. Churches can recognize allies, name enemies, remain neutral, or march into structured war.

**Diplomacy actions (leader only):**
- Set relations: Ally / Enemy / Neutral with other churches
- View current diplomatic standings in the Church GUI

**War system:**
- Declare war through the Church GUI
- Allies can join your war effort
- Objectives: destroy enemy cores, raid enemy vaults
- Outcomes: victory, draw, or surrender with terms
- Centralized notifications keep everyone informed

Before you start a war, make sure you know what you are risking.

---

### Lands Treasury Integration

Churches now connect more deeply with Lands.

Treasury linking lets church systems integrate with Land-based economies and territory management. Territory, money, and religion now pull on the same thread.

**To link:**
- Place the Church Core in a territory to link them
- Or use the GUI
- Or use /coi church commands and subcommands to link/unlink manually
---

### Legacy Church Migration

Existing churches are not abandoned. After founding with a core, players can be prompted through transfer flows that help preserve existing church progress where possible. Old faiths can cross into the new age instead of being buried by it.

---

### What To Watch

- **Church Status screen** — Members, Active Anchor Weight, Madness Floor, Anchors Needed
- **Inactive members** — shown in grey after 14 days; they don't count as anchors
- **Site health** — active sites = stronger church
- **Vault security** — protect it or lose it
- **Diplomatic standing** — know who your enemies are before they arrive
