### How to Send Items

1. **Right-click** with the Horn of Summoning. Your courier will appear beside you.
2. **Left-click** the courier to open its interface.
3. Place up to the allowed number of **items** into the courier's slots.
4. Click **Select Recipient** and choose the player you want to send to.
5. Click **Confirm Delivery** to dispatch the courier.

The sender and recipient will both receive a chat notification when the delivery starts and when it arrives.

---

### Offline Delivery

If your recipient is **offline** when you send, the items are queued. The courier will deliver automatically the next time they log in, once the original delivery timer has elapsed.

---

### Where Couriers Work

Couriers can be summoned and will deliver in the following dimensions:

- Overworld
- Nether
- Manipulation Pool
- Mirror Realm
- Nightmare Pool
- Secret Lair

Attempting to summon a courier in any other dimension (such as The End) will fail.
