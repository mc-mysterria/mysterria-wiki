## Crafting System

The mystical firearms crafting system is a multi-tier process that requires specialized components and a dedicated crafting station.

---

## Weaponsmith Table

The **Weaponsmith Table** is your weapon assembly station where you combine weapon parts to create complete firearms.

### Recipe

ðŸ“Œ **Ingredients:**
- 1 Smithing Table
- 4 Iron Blocks
- 4 Redstone Blocks

### How to Use

1. Place the Weaponsmith Table in your world
2. Gather all required weapon parts in your inventory
3. **Right-click** the table to view craftable weapons
4. **Shift + Right-Click** to assemble the weapon
5. The completed weapon appears in your inventory!

<Aside type="tip">
The Weaponsmith Table will only show weapons you have all the parts for. Missing parts? They won't appear in the crafting menu.
</Aside>

### Part Recipes

| Part | Recipe | Materials Needed |
|------|--------|------------------|
| **Repeater Barrel** | 3 Iron Ingots + 1 Lapis Lazuli | 3 Iron, 1 Lapis |
| **Repeater Cylinder** | 2 Iron Ingots + 1 Redstone | 2 Iron, 1 Redstone |
| **Repeater Frame** | 2 Iron Ingots + 1 Lapis Lazuli | 2 Iron, 1 Lapis |
| **Repeater Grip** | 1 Dark Oak Log + 2 Iron Ingots | 1 Dark Oak, 2 Iron |

### Total Materials Required

To craft one complete Klein's Repeater from scratch:

- **9 Iron Ingots**
- **2 Lapis Lazuli**
- **1 Redstone**
- **1 Dark Oak Log**

### Assembly Steps

1. Craft all 4 parts using a regular crafting table
2. Place the Weaponsmith Table
3. Ensure all 4 parts are in your inventory
4. Right-click the Weaponsmith Table
5. Shift + Right-Click to assemble Klein's Repeater

<Aside type="tip">
**Resource Efficiency Tip:** Before crafting multiple repeaters, ensure you have a steady supply of iron and lapis lazuli. Consider setting up an iron farm and mining at the right Y-levels for lapis!
</Aside>

## Deathknell Revolver & Steam-Pressured Rifle

These advanced weapons cannot be crafted from scratch. Their parts are obtained as **rewards from dungeons (nodes)**.

### How to Obtain Parts

1. Complete dungeon nodes on the server
2. Receive weapon parts as rewards
3. Collect all required parts for your desired weapon
4. Assemble at the Weaponsmith Table

<Aside type="caution">
**Node Rewards Only:** Deathknell Revolver and Steam-Pressured Rifle parts are exclusive rewards from dungeon completions. Plan your dungeon runs accordingly!
</Aside>

### Required Parts

**Deathknell Revolver:**
- Revolver Barrel
- Revolver Cylinder
- Revolver Frame
- Revolver Grip

**Steam-Pressured Rifle:**
- Rifle Barrel
- Rifle Stock
- Rifle Receiver
- Rifle Trigger Assembly

## Crafting Strategy

### For Beginners

1. **Start with Klein's Repeater** - It's craftable and effective
2. **Gather iron and lapis** - Set up basic resource collection
3. **Craft brass early** - You'll need it for ammunition
4. **Build the Weaponsmith Table** - Central to all weapon assembly

### For Advanced Players

1. **Farm dungeons** - Collect parts for advanced weapons
2. **Stockpile brass** - Special ammunition requires significant brass
3. **Maintain multiple weapons** - Each excels in different situations
4. **Optimize part storage** - Organize weapon parts for quick access
