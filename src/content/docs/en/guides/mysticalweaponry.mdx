## What is Mystical Weaponry?

Mystical Weaponry brings the legendary firearms from Lord of the Mysteries into Minecraft! Craft powerful weapons, load them with specialized ammunition, and experience a whole new dimension of combat.

## What You'll Need

To get started with mystical firearms:

1. **A Weaponsmith Table** - Special crafting station for assembling weapons
2. **Weapon Parts** - Components to build your weapon
3. **Ammunition** - Bullets to fire your weapon


## Available Weapons

### 1. Klein's Repeater

**Type:** Mystical Repeater

A balanced, reliable firearm perfect for beginners and versatile enough for experienced users.

- **Damage:** 4.0 per shot
- **Magazine Size:** 6 rounds
- **Range:** 40 blocks
- **Reload Time:** 3 seconds
- **Fire Rate:** 1 shot per second
- **Ammo Type:** Klein Rounds

---

### 2. Deathknell Revolver

**Type:** Heavy Revolver

A devastating hand cannon with exceptional stopping power.

- **Damage:** 8.0 per shot
- **Magazine Size:** 6 rounds
- **Range:** 60 blocks
- **Reload Time:** 0.8 seconds (loads one round at a time)
- **Fire Rate:** 0.9 seconds between shots
- **Ammo Type:** Deathknell Rounds

<Aside type="caution">Parts for this weapon are obtained as **rewards from dungeons (nodes)**. Features one-by-one reload mechanics for tactical shell management.</Aside>

---

### 3. Steam-Pressured Rifle

**Type:** Long-Range Precision Rifle

A steam-powered masterpiece designed for long-distance elimination.

- **Damage:** 14.0 per shot
- **Magazine Size:** 5 rounds
- **Range:** 100 blocks
- **Reload Time:** 4 seconds
- **Fire Rate:** 1.5 seconds between shots
- **Ammo Type:** Rifle Rounds

<Aside type="caution">Parts for this weapon are obtained as **rewards from dungeons (nodes)**. Features authentic Kar98k-style reload mechanics.</Aside>

## How to Use Weapons

### Basic Controls

| Action | Control |
|--------|---------|
| **Fire** | Right-Click |
| **Reload** | Left-Click |

### Firing Your Weapon

1. Hold the weapon in your hand
2. Ensure you have ammunition loaded
3. Aim at your target
4. Right-click to fire
5. Watch the action bar for your current ammo count

### Understanding the HUD

The action bar (above your hotbar) shows:
- **Current Ammo / Max Ammo** (e.g., "Klein's Repeater 4/6")
- **Reload Status** (yellow "Reloading..." during reload)
- **Ammo Type** (colored text showing your current ammunition effect)
- **Special Warnings** (red "No ammo!" when empty)

## Ammunition Types

Every weapon has **13 different ammunition variants** with unique effects. All ammo types use the same base damage as the weapon, but special rounds have **damage modifiers** and **mystical effects**.

### Standard Ammunition

| Ammo Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Klein Rounds** | Standard ammunition (100% damage) |
| **Deathknell Rounds** | Standard revolver ammunition (100% damage) |
| **Rifle Rounds** | Standard rifle ammunition (100% damage) |

## Combat Tips

<Aside type="tip">**Accuracy Matters:** Your accuracy decreases when moving. Stand still for best precision (Stationary = Perfect, Moving = 4¬∞ spread, In Air = 8¬∞ spread)</Aside>

‚ú® **Ammo Management:**
- Carry multiple ammo types for different situations

‚ú® **Reload Timing:**

‚ú® **Visual Feedback:**
