## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Fool Pathway?
The main advantages are:
- Diversity of tactical abilities
- Decent attacking abilities
- Unique evasion and deception mechanics
- Flexibility in various situations

### What are the disadvantages of the Fool Pathway?
- Limited attacking power
- Complexity in masterful use of all abilities
- Need for a creative approach to each situation

### What playstyle fits best?
The Fool Pathway is ideal for players who enjoy cunning tactics, unexpected moves, and deceptive maneuvers. This is a choice for those who want to be masters of unpredictability, outplaying opponents with their mind rather than brute force.
