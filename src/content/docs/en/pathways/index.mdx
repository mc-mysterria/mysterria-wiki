Choose your unique character development path with special abilities and possibilities.

## Available Pathways:

### ☀️ Sun Pathway
- Healing abilities
- Protective spells
- Light magic

### 🌙 Moon Pathway
- Illusions and deceptions
- Night vision
- Dark magic

### ⚔️ Warrior Pathway
- Combat abilities
- Enhanced endurance
- Weapon mastery

### 🧙 Mage Pathway
- Powerful spells
- Magical research
- Energy control

### 🗡️ Assassin Pathway
- Stealth and speed
- Critical strikes
- Poison attacks

## Pathway Selection

Each Pathway has unique advantages and development features. Choose the one that best fits your playstyle!
