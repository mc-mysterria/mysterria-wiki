## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Error Pathway?
The main advantages are:
- Ability to decipher knowledge and information
- Possibility to steal things and abilities
- Possibility to create copies to distract opponents
- Unique temporary teleportation abilities

### What are the disadvantages of the Error Pathway?
- Lower direct combat power compared to attacking pathways
- Need for preparation and planning for effective ability usage
- Some abilities require certain conditions for use

### What playstyle fits best?
The Error Pathway is ideal for players who value preparation and enjoy theft. This is a path for those who seek to defeat their enemies with cunning and foreign powers.
