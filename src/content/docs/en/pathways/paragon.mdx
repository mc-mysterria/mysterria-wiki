### Main Abilities:
- 🛠️ Unique ability to create artifacts and items
- 🌠 Attacking abilities related to starlight
- 🤖 Creation of artificial life
- 🔎 Appraisal abilities

### Sequences (9-5 levels):

- **Level 9:** Scholar
- **Level 8:** Archaeologist
- **Level 7:** Appraiser
- **Level 6:** Artisan
- **Level 5:** Astronomer
- **Level 4:** Alchemist

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Paragon Pathway?
The main advantages are:
- Strong movement skills already at low sequences
- Universal appraisal skills
- Strong attacking skills at high sequences
- Ability to forge artifacts

### What are the disadvantages of the Paragon Pathway?
- Lack of attacking abilities until high sequences
- High cost of artifact creation
- Lack of control skills

### What playstyle fits best?
The Paragon Pathway is more suited for those who want to have a balanced playstyle - combining creation and research with the ability to become a dangerous force at high levels, and the possibility to create powerful artifacts.
