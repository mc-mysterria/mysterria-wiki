## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Door Pathway?
The main advantages are:
- Unique mobility and ability to pass through obstacles
- Control over space and teleportation
- Powerful escape abilities and distracting abilities
- Direct attacking abilities and the possibility to record others' abilities

### What are the disadvantages of the Door Pathway?
- Lower attack power compared to combat pathways
- More complex mechanics of ability usage

### What playstyle fits best?
The Door Pathway is ideal for explorer players who value freedom of movement and the ability to avoid problems instead of direct confrontation. This is the path of those who never want to be caught and love to travel in comfort.
