## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Hanged Man Pathway?
The main advantages are:
- Powerful flesh manipulation abilities
- Ability to absorb abilities of other beings
- Unique hiding and masking skills
- Ability to use shadows to control enemies

### What are the disadvantages of the Hanged Man Pathway?
- Abilities require sacrifices from the player or their companions
- Some techniques have high risk of fatal consequences
- Weakness at low sequences

### What playstyle fits best?
The Hanged Man Pathway is ideal for players who value ambushes, cunning, and tactical approach to battle. This is a path for those who are ready to risk and sacrifice their own health for power, who seek to control the battlefield and capture the souls of their enemies.
