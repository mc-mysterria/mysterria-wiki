If you are a content creator looking to grow your channel while playing on Mysterria, you may be eligible to join our Creator Program.

We value creators of all sizes who are passionate about our community. Joining the program grants you the **Author** rank (and specific tag in-game), which comes with perks designed to help you produce better content and stand out.

- **Boon**: An **Eternal Edict** pathway Sequence 8 boon, available only to creators.
- **Support:** Access to a private creators channel in Discord for communication with staff. In case you need personal assistance, we will be there.

## âš–ï¸ Rules & Expectations

Creators are the face of the Mysterria community. We hold you to a higher standard than the average player.

1.  **Good Standing:** You must not have any recent bans or mutes on the server or Discord.
2.  **Family Friendly-ish:** While we allow mild language, excessive toxicity, slurs, or controversial topics are strictly prohibited.
3.  **Representation:** You are a role model. If you are found breaking rules, exploiting glitches for content, or harassing players, your rank will be removed immediately.
4.  **Activity:** If you stop uploading Mysterria content for more than 30 days without notice, your rank may be removed.
