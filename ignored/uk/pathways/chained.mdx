:::tip[Reference]
Chained represents restraint, imprisonment, and psychological manipulation, granting control over minds and emotions.
:::

### Main Abilities

- Psychological manipulation and control
- Restraint and binding abilities
- Emotion detection and influence
- Mental torture and interrogation

### Sequences (Levels 9-4)

- **Level 9:** Prisoner
- **Level 8:** Lunatic
- **Level 7:** Werewolf
- **Level 6:** Zombie
- **Level 5:** Wraith
- **Level 4:** Puppet

### Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Chained Pathway?

The main advantages are:
- Powerful psychological manipulation
- Restraint and control abilities
- Mental interrogation skills

### What are the disadvantages of the Chained Pathway?

- Sequence curses

### What is the best playstyle?

The Chained Pathway is ideal for players who enjoy warfare, mental manipulation, and controlling enemies through their minds and emotions. It's perfect for those who want to break their enemies mentally rather than physically.
