:::tip[Reference]
Death represents the connection between life and death, granting control over spirits and the undead.
:::

### Main Abilities

- Communication with spirits and the dead
- Necromancy and undead manipulation
- Soul-based abilities

### Sequences (Levels 9-4)

- **Level 9:** Corpse Collector
- **Level 8:** Gravedigger
- **Level 7:** Spirit Medium
- **Level 6:** Spirit Guide
- **Level 5:** Gatekeeper
- **Level 4:** Undying

### Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Death Pathway?

The main advantages are:
- Control over spirits and undead creatures
- Communication with the deceased for information
- Strong life force manipulation
- Resistance to death-based attacks

### What are the disadvantages of the Death Pathway?

- Limited effectiveness against non-living enemies
- Requires corpses or spirits nearby
- Socially stigmatized abilities

### What is the best playstyle?

The Death Pathway is ideal for players who enjoy necromancy, spirit communication, and controlling undead forces. It's perfect for those who want to command an army of spirits and manipulate the boundary between life and death.
