### Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Sun Pathway?
The main advantages are:
- Powerful attacking abilities against groups of enemies
- Ability to support self and allies
- Protection against negative effects
- Empowerment during the day

### What are the disadvantages of the Sun Pathway?
- Lack of control skills
- Weak mobility
- Less effective in dungeons and at night

### What is the best playstyle?
The Sun Pathway is ideal for players who want to protect allies, destroy enemies with powerful abilities, and bring light to the darkest corners of the world. It is a straightforward path for those who value justice and strength.
