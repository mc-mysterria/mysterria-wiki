:::tip[Reference]
White Tower represents erudition, knowledge, and scholarly pursuits, granting enhanced learning and analytical abilities.
:::

### Main Abilities

- Enhanced memory and learning capabilities
- Knowledge accumulation and retention
- Analytical and deduction skills
- Research and investigation abilities

### Sequences (Levels 9-4)

- **Level 9:** Reader
- **Level 8:** Spirit Medium
- **Level 7:** Detective
- **Level 6:** Polymath
- **Level 5:** Mysticism Magister
- **Level 4:** Prophet

### Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the White Tower Pathway?

The main advantages are:
- Exceptional analytical and deduction capabilities
- Vast knowledge accumulation
- Enhanced learning speed
- Strong research and investigation skills

### What are the disadvantages of the White Tower Pathway?

- Limited direct combat abilities
- Reliance on preparation and knowledge
- Weaker physical capabilities
- Requires extensive study and research

### What is the best playstyle?

The White Tower Pathway is ideal for players who enjoy strategic planning, research, and using knowledge to overcome challenges. It's perfect for those who prefer to outsmart opponents rather than overpower them with brute force.
