:::tip[Reference]
Hermit represents hidden knowledge, secrets, and esoteric wisdom, granting access to mysterious and arcane powers.
:::

### Main Abilities

- Access to hidden and forbidden knowledge
- Esoteric and arcane abilities
- Information gathering and secrets
- Mystical perception and insight

### Sequences (Levels 9-4)

- **Level 9:** Mystery Pryer
- **Level 8:** Melee Scholar
- **Level 7:** Warlock
- **Level 6:** Scroll Professor
- **Level 5:** Constellations Master
- **Level 4:** Mysticologist

### Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Hermit Pathway?

The main advantages are:
- Access to vast hidden knowledge and secrets
- Powerful arcane and esoteric abilities
- Strong information gathering capabilities
- Mystical insight and perception

### What are the disadvantages of the Hermit Pathway?

- Knowledge can be dangerous and corrupting
- Limited direct combat abilities early on
- Requires extensive study and research
- Risk of madness from forbidden knowledge

### What is the best playstyle?

The Hermit Pathway is ideal for players who enjoy uncovering secrets, gathering hidden knowledge, and wielding arcane powers. It's perfect for those who seek to understand the mysteries of the world and use esoteric wisdom to their advantage.
