:::tip[Reference]
Moon represents crimson moon, blood, and transformation, granting vampiric and lunar abilities.
:::

### Main Abilities

- Blood manipulation and control
- Lunar-based powers
- Transformation abilities
- Enhanced physical capabilities at night

### Sequences (Levels 9-4)

- **Level 9:** Apothecary
- **Level 8:** Beast Tamer
- **Level 7:** Vampire

- **Level 5:** Scarlet Scholar
- **Level 4:** Shaman King

### Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Moon Pathway?

The main advantages are:
- Powerful blood manipulation abilities
- Enhanced strength during nighttime
- Potion crafting and alchemical skills
- Life-draining capabilities

### What are the disadvantages of the Moon Pathway?

- Weaker during daytime
- Blood dependency for some abilities
- Vulnerability to purification
- Potential loss of humanity

### What is the best playstyle?

The Moon Pathway is ideal for players who enjoy vampiric powers, potion crafting, and lunar-based abilities. It's perfect for those who want to harness the power of blood and moonlight to dominate their enemies.
