### Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Red Priest Pathway?
The main advantages are:
- High attacking potential, especially using fire abilities
- Ability to provoke enemies and control battle
- Fire resistance and immunity to own explosions
- Unique long-range attacking abilities

### What are the disadvantages of the Red Priest Pathway?
- Lack of mobility
- Risk of harming allies when using explosive abilities
- Lack of debuffs or direct enemy control

### What playstyle fits best?
The Red Priest Pathway is ideal for aggressive players who enjoy dealing massive damage, controlling the battlefield, and having advantages in direct confrontations. This is a path for those who prefer attack power over defense and are ready to risk for achieving devastating victory.
