Abyss represents demonic power, chaos, and destruction, granting devastating offensive abilities.

### Main Abilities

- Destructive demonic powers
- Corruption and degeneration abilities
- Chaotic energy manipulation
- Enhanced physical strength through demonic transformation

### Sequences (Levels 9-4)

- **Level 9:** Criminal
- **Level 8:** Coldblooded
- **Level 7:** Serial Killer
- **Level 6:** Devil
- **Level 5:** Desire Apostle
- **Level 4:** Fallen

### Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Abyss Pathway?

The main advantages are:
- Extremely high destructive power
- Powerful corruption abilities
- Strong offensive capabilities
- Enhanced physical attributes through demonic power

### What are the disadvantages of the Abyss Pathway?

- High risk of corruption and madness
- Loss of humanity, you will have to kill others to survive
- Difficult to control destructive urges
- Socially stigmatized and feared

### What is the best playstyle?

The Abyss Pathway is ideal for players who enjoy raw destructive power, demonic abilities, and embracing chaos. It's perfect for those willing to sacrifice their humanity for devastating offensive capabilities and the power to bring ruin to their enemies.
