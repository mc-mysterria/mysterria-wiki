:::tip[Reference]
Justiciar represents justice, judgment, and punishment, granting abilities to enforce fairness and punish wrongdoing.
:::

### Main Abilities

- Judgment and punishment abilities
- Truth detection and lie identification
- Justice enforcement
- Law-based combat skills

### Sequences (Levels 9-4)

- **Level 9:** Arbiter
- **Level 8:** Sheriff
- **Level 7:** Interrogator
- **Level 6:** Judge
- **Level 5:** Disciplinary Paladin
- **Level 4:** Imperative Mage

### Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the main advantages of the Justiciar Pathway?

The main advantages are:
- Powerful abilities against rule-breakers and criminals
- Truth detection and interrogation skills
- Strong combat abilities for enforcing justice
- Bonus damage against those who violate established rules

### What are the disadvantages of the Justiciar Pathway?

- Effectiveness depends on rule violations
- Rigid moral code requirements
- Limited utility outside combat and judgment
- Weaker against those who follow rules

### What is the best playstyle?

The Justiciar Pathway is ideal for players who enjoy enforcing justice, judging others, and punishing wrongdoers. It's perfect for those who want to be arbiters of fairness and wield the power of judgment.
